About

Rohith Vishal

Photographer

STUDIO INFINITE Wedding photography services began their journey in the year 2017. Our founder Master. Rohith Vishal believed that Wedding photography doesn’t have to be conventional and unimaginative which led to our works of art that will blow your mind, that will communicate the essence of who you are, that will tell stories beyond what you see, and that makes even strangers pause and gaze.

Our mission that wedding photography wasn't simply about selling a service, rather it is to preserve the moments cherished in pictures. And from then It could’ve never been any better than satisfying our clients with our unsurpassed work.

Our services range from weddings, engagement portraits, family portraits, Children’s milestones, and parties, to community sporting events, professional headshots, and corporate events. We got you Focused!
